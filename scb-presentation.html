SCB Date Box Gifting

Dashboard overview & section breakdown

1

Introduction

This document walks through the gifting dashboard built for SCB. The dashboard gives a clear, real-time view of date box delivery progress — overall completion, city-wise breakdown, status pipeline, and a full searchable recipient list. It is accessible at scb.tw-traders.com.

Date boxes delivered — total progress

A single progress bar at the top of the dashboard showing the percentage of date boxes that have been Delivered out of the total number of recipients. This is the primary KPI for how far along the gifting campaign is.

Total progress bar

4

City-wise delivery breakdown

Individual progress bars for each city. For every city it shows how many boxes are delivered versus the total (e.g. "12 / 20 — 60%"). This lets SCB quickly identify which regions are ahead of schedule and which need attention.

City-wise progress

5

Status-wise breakdown

Four status cards showing how many boxes are at each stage of the pipeline. The counts always add up to the total number of recipients and update automatically as statuses change.

🌴

In Production

Date boxes currently being prepared at the farm.

🔍

Quality Check

Boxes received at the warehouse and undergoing quality inspection.

📦

Packed

Boxes packed, labelled, and ready for dispatch.

✅

Delivered

Successfully delivered to the recipient.

All deliveries — recipient table

A table listing every recipient with their Name, Company/Designation, Address, City and current Status. Users can search by name, contact or address, and filter by city and status to quickly find any entry.
